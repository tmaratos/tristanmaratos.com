How this compares

Three views of where custom development fits relative to other options.

Many businesses today use DIY website builders or template based platforms to get online quickly. Those solutions can work well for simple needs, but custom built websites provide greater flexibility, scalability, performance, and long term customization.

Option 01

DIY website builders

  • Faster initial setup
  • Lower upfront cost
  • Subscription based pricing
  • Template driven layouts
  • Limited customization flexibility
  • Platform lock in over time

Option 02

Traditional web agencies

  • Larger team structures
  • Higher project overhead
  • Strong formal support processes
  • Multi step approval workflows
  • Typically higher project pricing
  • Less direct developer access

Option 03

Custom development by Tristan Maratos

  • Flexible custom architecture
  • Direct developer communication
  • Tailored functionality per project
  • Modern administrative systems available
  • Adaptable long term as the business grows
  • No template or platform lock in
